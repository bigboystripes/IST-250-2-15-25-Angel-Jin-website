About Us

Hello, we are the sunday love Club. We are a band based in State College, PA . We are all from the local area, and in fact did not meet in the university
Instead, we met in highschool, and began to write compositions together in the year of 2023. We blend a mixture of many different genres and styles, but our current material could be described as shoegazey with a bit of 70s rock influence as well.
